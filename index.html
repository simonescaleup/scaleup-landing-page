Meet the Founder

Simone Patel is the founder of ScaleUp Partners, a consultancy born out of real-world experience and a passion for transformation. Having previously owned a successful business supplying ICU and theater consumables to high-end hospitals across Africa, Simone witnessed firsthand the operational challenges — and untapped opportunities — within the healthcare space.

That journey revealed a clear gap in the market: while health-focused companies were innovating in care, few were leveraging AI and data-driven strategies to scale their client base or increase profitability. Simone founded ScaleUp Partners to bridge that gap — helping HealthTech and Telehealth firms harness cutting-edge AI tools, refined pricing strategies, and proven marketing systems to unlock serious growth.

Today, she partners with innovative teams around the world to scale their revenue, streamline operations, and build a brand that truly leads in the health sector.
